# Community-Service-Tracker

Overview
The Community Service Tracker is an online platform designed to help students log, track, and manage their community service hours efficiently. With an intuitive interface, it simplifies monitoring initial service hours, logging completed hours, and managing additional assignments due to disciplinary actions. 

Key Features
Initial Hours Allocation:
  Every student starts with a default of 50 community service hours.
Real-Time Hour Tracking:
  Students can log hours completed and view their remaining hours in real time.
Punishment Hour Management:
  Admins/teachers can assign additional hours for disciplinary reasons, and the system reflects updated totals seamlessly.
Service Opportunities Database:
  Lists approved places where students can fulfill their community service obligations.
Verification and Approval:
  Hours submitted by students are reviewed and finalized by admins/teachers.
  Approval is mandatory to ensure the validity and authenticity of completed service hours, including the initial 50.
User Dashboard:
  Personalized dashboards for students to track progress and for admins to manage logs.
